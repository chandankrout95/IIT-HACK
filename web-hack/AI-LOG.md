### A. Mathematical Refinement
The **Neural Risk Engine** calculates a threat score using non-linear variables. LLMs were used to refine the mathematical syntax of the trajectory projections within the `AsteroidGraphModal.jsx`, ensuring the Recharts visualization correctly rendered the decay of distance over time.






### B. Component Boilerplate
To maintain a rapid development pace, AI was used to generate initial functional skeletons for repetitive UI elements (e.g., `QuickStat` components). This allowed the human developers to focus on the complex **NASA NeoWs** data parsing and **Socket.io** event handling.

### C. Debugging & Error Handling
AI was leveraged to interpret obscure error logs from the **Three.js** renderer and to provide optimized `try/catch` blocks for the NASA API uplink to ensure system stability during network fluctuations.
